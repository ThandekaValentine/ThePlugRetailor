Privacy Policy

POPIA-compliant. Your privacy matters to us.

Last updated: June 2026. The Plug Retailor ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full name, email address, and phone number provided during checkout or contact forms
  • Delivery address, city, province, and postal code
  • Order history and preferences (stored locally in your browser)
  • Payment information entered at checkout (processed securely; we do not store full card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To communicate with you about your order status
  • To respond to enquiries submitted via our contact form or WhatsApp
  • To send marketing communications, only if you opt in via our newsletter

3. Local Storage & Cookies

This website uses your browser's Local Storage to save your cart, wishlist, and recently viewed items. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you complete an order.

4. Third-Party Services

We use EmailJS to send order notifications to our team. When you place an order, relevant order and contact details are transmitted securely to this service for the sole purpose of order processing.

5. Your Rights Under POPIA

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • The right to object to the processing of your personal information
  • The right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your POPIA rights, please contact us or email info@theplugretailor.co.za.
