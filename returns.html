Returns & Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent returns.

At The Plug Retailor, we want you to love every purchase. If something isn't right, here's how our returns process works.

1. Return Window

You may request a return within 7 days of receiving your order, for both new and pre-loved items.

2. Eligibility for Returns

  • New items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached.
  • Pre-loved items must be returned in the same condition as described on the listing (referencing the condition rating: Like New, Excellent, Good, or Fair).
  • Items must not show signs of additional wear, damage, or alteration after delivery.

3. Non-Returnable Items

  • Items marked "Final Sale"
  • Earrings and other hygiene-sensitive accessories (unless faulty)
  • Gift cards or vouchers

4. How to Request a Return

Contact us via WhatsApp or email at info@theplugretailor.co.za with your order number and reason for return. Our team will guide you through the next steps within 24–48 hours.

5. Refunds

Once your returned item is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval status. Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

6. Pre-Loved Item Discrepancies

If a pre-loved item's condition differs materially from what was listed, you are entitled to a full refund including original delivery costs.

7. Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. Please return the original item for a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

8. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us immediately with photos so we can resolve the issue quickly at no cost to you.

Questions about a return? Contact our support team or chat with us on WhatsApp.
